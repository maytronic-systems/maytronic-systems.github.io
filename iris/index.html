Product by Maytronic Systems

IRIS

IRIS is an Android software product that makes app permissions, network traffic, and activity-related information easier to inspect through structured, readable views.

Get IRIS Explore features

IRIS is presented as one concrete product within the broader Maytronic Systems portfolio of software services and digital solutions.

IRIS features

Focused analytics for app-related visibility.

IRIS is organized around practical inspection tasks so users can review relevant signals without unnecessary complexity.

Permission monitor

Review granted permissions per app with grouped, readable summaries designed for fast comparison.

Network activity views

Inspect traffic-oriented information through structured dashboards that help identify network-heavy apps.

Usage and activity insights

Understand app activity patterns with views built to surface foreground, background, and timeline-related signals.

What IRIS is built for

  • Readable inspection of technical app metadata
  • Structured dashboards instead of noisy raw listings
  • Analysis-oriented presentation for practical decision support

How it fits the portfolio

IRIS shows how Maytronic Systems approaches product development: technical scope, strong information hierarchy, and implementation that turns a complex use case into a usable interface.
